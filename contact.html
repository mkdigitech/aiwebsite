Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working with us

How quickly can you start working on my project?

Once we've had an initial consultation and agreed on the scope of work, we can typically begin within 1-2 weeks. For urgent projects, we may be able to accommodate a faster timeline.

What information do you need to provide a quote?

To provide an accurate quote, we'll need information about your business goals, target audience, current marketing efforts, desired services, and project timeline. The more details you can provide, the more precise our quote will be.

Do you offer ongoing support after project completion?

Yes, we offer various support and maintenance packages to ensure your marketing efforts continue to perform optimally. Our team can provide regular updates, performance monitoring, and strategic adjustments as needed.

How do you measure the success of your marketing campaigns?

We establish clear KPIs at the beginning of each project and provide regular reports on these metrics. Depending on your goals, we might track website traffic, conversion rates, lead generation, social engagement, or other relevant metrics. Our AI tools allow for detailed attribution and performance analysis.

Do you work with businesses outside of India?

Yes, we work with clients globally. Our team is experienced in international marketing strategies and can adapt our approach to different markets and cultural contexts.
