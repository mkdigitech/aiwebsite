Our Story

MKDigital was founded in 2015 with a vision to revolutionize the digital marketing landscape in India through the power of artificial intelligence. What began as a small team of three passionate marketers and AI enthusiasts has grown into India's leading AI marketing agency.

Our journey started when our founders, Manish Kumar and Kavita Sharma, recognized the untapped potential of AI in transforming traditional marketing approaches. They believed that by combining cutting-edge AI technology with creative marketing strategies, they could help businesses achieve unprecedented growth and success in the digital realm.

Today, MKDigital stands as a testament to that vision. We've helped hundreds of businesses across various industries leverage the power of AI to enhance their marketing efforts, increase their ROI, and establish a strong digital presence. Our commitment to innovation, excellence, and client success has earned us the reputation of being the No.1 AI Marketing Agency in India.
